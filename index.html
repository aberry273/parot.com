FAQ

Why 'parot'?

We loved Twitter and wanted to create an alternative that led to better discussions. Unfortunately the 'Parrot' domain was a lot more expensive..

Is there only a single feed?

We have a single feed, however users can create their own channels which can contain their own posts or aggregate posts that share the same tags and catergories as their channel.

What about Threads/BlueSky?

We see this as clones of Twitter. We want to make something a bit different and see providing users control over their own threads for discussions as being a key element to do so.

Why choose this over Twitter or Reddit?

Our objective is to drive better real-time discussions between users. We like the discussion aspect of Reddit but have the interface, we like the interface and creative energy of Twitter, but hate the discussions. So we're trying to find a common ground between the two platforms that enables the best of both worlds.

What about AI

We want to exclude bots and AI based conversation as much as possible. We will work hard to ensure content posts by AI is flagged to allow consumers to easily identify it and vote on it accordingly
